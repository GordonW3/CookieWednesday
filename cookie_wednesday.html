Celebrate life’s moments with a little sugar!

Several years ago, we had just moved into a new home and a new development. We were all trying to get to know each other, so I started a new tradition. I made cookies every Wednesday after school. Moms and kids were invited over. The kids took turns choosing what kind. It didn’t take long for the word to spread and soon we became great friends with our neighbors, who I love dearly . . . and Cookie Wednesday began.
